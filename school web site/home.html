  • Call for Participation in Antalya Organized Industrial Zone R&D and Innovation Project Market
    • It started the application process of the Antalya OIZ R&D and Innovation Project Market 2022 Event, which will be held by the Antalya Organized Industrial Zone in order to increase the added value in production, bring new technologies and methods to the industry, strengthen university-industry cooperation, and spread the R&D and innovation culture. A project competition and exhibition will be held as part of the event. Participants, 'Agriculture, Chemistry, Environment, Biotechnology (Agricultural and Animal), Food' – 'Energy, Machinery, Electric - Electronic, Construction, Software, Informatics' and 'Artificial Intelligence, Health and Medical Technologies, Biomaterials, Pharmaceuticals and Cosmetics' They will be able to apply to the competition with their R&D and innovation-based project proposals they have developed in 3 different categories.
